# Northern Shield — Economy

*Many resources, many decisions — gold is only the battle blood*

---

## Philosophy

> **Gold wins fights. Wood, stone, and iron build the North. Reputation decides who fights with you. Ancient knowledge decides what is possible.**

Avoid **gold solves everything**. Each layer has a **job** and a **ceiling**.

---

## Layer 1 — Battle economy (in assault)

**Resource:** Gold (◆)

| Source | Sink |
|--------|------|
| Enemy kills | Emergency repairs (if any mid-fight — target: none in campaign) |
| Wave clear | *(campaign: minimal sinks during battle)* |
| Events | |

**Design role:** Immediate tactical feedback — *the fight paid for itself*. Between assaults, unspent battle gold flows to **field state** or **reserve** per rules.

**Principle:** Battle gold should not **skip** campaign progression (no “save 50k and skip Fortress tier”).

---

## Layer 2 — Campaign economy (between assaults)

**Resources:** Wood · Stone · Iron · Food

| Resource | Job | Typical sinks |
|----------|-----|----------------|
| **Wood** | Growth, repair, palisade era | Palisade repair, barracks, recruit housing |
| **Stone** | Permanent walls, halls | Wall tier, Great Hall, gate reinforcement |
| **Iron** | War machines, arms | Siege platforms, armor crafting, gate fixtures |
| **Food** | People eat | Roster upkeep, recruit attract, morale events |

### Generation (design targets)
- Assault victory → bundle by region difficulty
- War Camp buildings → passive trickle
- Events / caravans → spikes
- Failure → **debt** (food shortage narrative, not instant game over)

### Storage
- **Treasury** → gold reserve cap (exists in prototype)
- **Granary / warehouse** → food & material caps (planned)
- Overflow → waste or tribute event (choice)

---

## Layer 3 — Kingdom progress (slow, identity)

**Resources:** Reputation · Ancient Knowledge

| Resource | Job |
|----------|-----|
| **Reputation** | Allies, trade, recruit quality, diplomacy branches |
| **Ancient Knowledge** | Research nodes, rune walls, fortress spells, myth tech |

These should feel **rare** — earned from boss assaults, saga milestones, exploration nodes.

| Building | Unlocks | Primary resource |
|----------|---------|------------------|
| Tiny Treasury | Gold reserve | Gold |
| Granary | Food cap, roster upkeep | Wood + food |
| Quarry | Stone income | Food (workers) |
| Mine | Iron income | Food + stone |

| Forge | Crafting | Iron |
| Rune Forge | Rune tiers | Knowledge + iron |

| Scriptorium | Research | Knowledge |

## Stars (campaign meta — prototype)

**Stars (✦)** remain a **run-wide tactical currency** for runes and elite unlocks. Design direction:

- Stars are **not** a fourth gold
- Stars reward **assault performance**, not grinding
- Star sinks tie to **fortress magic** layer in Citadel phase

---

## Player tensions (intentional)

1. **Repair west gate** (stone) vs **recruit** (food + gold)
2. **Upgrade Hall** (stone) vs **forge sword** (iron)
3. **Spend reputation** for aid vs **hoard** for ending branch
4. **Research rune wall** vs **immediate wall repair** before boss

Every tension should map to **fortress fantasy**, not arbitrary timers.

---

## Anti-patterns

- Single currency with different labels
- Gold purchase of stone/iron without trade building
- Permanent upgrades only in battle shop
- Resource that only increments (no cap, no waste)
