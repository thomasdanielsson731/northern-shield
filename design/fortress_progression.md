# Northern Shield — Fortress Progression

*How the home grows from palisade to legend*

The fortress is not a static arena. It is a **character arc** — same location, radically different silhouette, systems, and emotional weight.

---

## Design rule

> **Every tier must be visible without opening a menu.**

Players should feel progression when panning the command map, entering War Camp, or opening Fortress Prep — not only when reading a +15% wall HP tooltip.

---

## Tier 0 — Campfire (prologue fantasy)

*Not a playable tier — emotional baseline.*

- Mud, fire pit, no name
- Establishes contrast for everything after

---

## Tier 1 — Village (start state)

### Visual identity
- Single wooden **palisade** ring
- One **gate** facing the threat
- **Watch tower** — lean timber, fire basket
- **Tiny treasury** — buried chest, not a hall
- Smoke from one longhouse

### Defensive identity
- **One Berserker** (named starter) holds the gate
- Palisade blocks nothing mythic — only raids
- Breach = visceral fear (wall splinters)

### Systems unlocked
- Basic War Camp
- Single-post fortress prep
- Battle gold
- First chronicle entry

### Player sentence
*"We are not safe — but we are here."*

---

## Tier 2 — Settlement

### Visual identity
- Palisade **reinforced** with stone footings
- Second gate stub (sealed until upgraded)
- **Barracks** smoke; **granary** silhouette
- Watch tower becomes **stone base**

### Defensive identity
- 3–6 heroes, multiple posts
- Food pressure appears (see [economy.md](economy.md))
- First **facing damage** persists (west wall scarred)

### Systems unlocked
- Multi-post assignment
- Fortress roles
- Wood + food spending
- Recruit cadence

### Player sentence
*"People come to us for shelter."*

---

## Tier 3 — Fortress

### Visual identity
- **Stone curtain wall** with crenellations
- Four gates on compass (one primary per assault)
- **Great Hall** at center — visible goal structure
- **Treasury** annex; **forge** chimney
- Banner on main tower

### Defensive identity
- Siege engines on platforms
- Gate HP per facing; repair choices between assaults
- Assault chains (2–3 waves) test **plan durability**

### Systems unlocked
- Meta buildings (Hall, Treasury, Forge)
- Stone / iron economy
- Promotion titles
- Scars on fortress + heroes

### Player sentence
*"This is a place on the map — enemies plan for us."*

---

## Tier 4 — Stronghold

### Visual identity
- **Outer bailey** + inner keep
- Market stalls, training yard, memorial stones
- Wall **faces** show repair history (patch stone, new timber)
- Allied flags on outer wall

### Defensive identity
- Multiple fronts on command map **feel different** (art + intel)
- Reputation affects aid (reinforcement wave, supply caravan)

### Systems unlocked
- Research
- Crafting tiers
- Diplomacy (light)
- Equipment slots beyond runes

### Player sentence
*"Kings know our name."*

---

## Tier 5 — Citadel

### Visual identity
- **Rune-etched walls** — faint glow at night
- Spires, bridge to watch peaks
- Dragon perch (empty until threat)
- Living city glow — windows, forges, ritual circle

### Defensive identity
- Fortress spells woven into wall faces
- Mythic assault modifiers (storm, curse, giant)
- Prep includes **spell loadout** and geas costs

### Systems unlocked
- Rune walls
- Fortress spell slots
- Hero spells at legend rank
- Dragon defense platforms

### Player sentence
*"Magic returned because we refused to die."*

---

## Tier 6 — Last Bastion (capstone)

### Visual identity
- Unique **campaign-path silhouette** (choices matter)
- Aurora behind spires; world-tree motif optional
- Memorial avenue of fallen legends
- Wall segments named in saga

### Defensive identity
- Final regions assume **citadel-grade** prep
- Failure mode is **narrative extinction**, not retry annoyance
- Victory preserves **scarred beauty** — not pristine CGI

### Systems unlocked
- Legacy succession
- Epilogue world states
- Saga export / chronicle book

### Player sentence
*"The North stands because of us."*

---

## Progression axes (all tiers)

| Axis | Village | Citadel |
|------|---------|---------|
| Wall material | Wood | Rune stone |
| Hero count felt | 1 | Warband + legends |
| Prep complexity | One gate | Posts + spells + craft |
| Threat tone | Raid | Mythic siege |
| UI density (battle) | Minimal | Still minimal |
| Story output | Names | Saga chapters |

---

## Visual evolution checklist (art direction)

- [ ] Palisade → stone → rune stone **silhouette change**
- [ ] Great Hall grows **three visible tiers**
- [ ] Gate models show **damage states** (intact / cracked / breached)
- [ ] War Camp backdrop **matches fortress tier**
- [ ] Command map fortress icon **scales with tier**
- [ ] Seasonal weather on fortress (optional polish)

---

## Relationship to defensive posts

Posts (West Gate, Watch Tower, etc.) are **labels on the evolving fortress**, not grid coordinates. As tiers advance, **post count and quality** grow; the player still thinks *“Gunnar holds the West Gate”* — the gate just becomes more magnificent.

See [DEFENSIVE_POSTS.md](DEFENSIVE_POSTS.md) for implementation spec.
