# Northern Shield — Hero Progression Tree

*Legends, not statistics — every class earns a saga*

Heroes answer two questions: **who are you?** (legend rank) and **how do you fight?** (class). Ranks are **shared**; class fantasies are **unique**. No balance numbers — only responsibilities, visuals, abilities, and fortress bonds.

**Combat archetypes mapped:**

| Archetype | Primary classes | Fortress question |
|-----------|-----------------|-------------------|
| Tank | Valkyrie, Berserker (late) | Who absorbs the breach? |
| Archer | Military, Blondie | Who strikes first from safety? |
| Support | Hydda | Who keeps the warband fed and ready? |
| Healer | Valkyrie (path), Temple Healer | Who brings them back from the edge? |
| Mage | Blondie (path), Skald | Who bends weather and runes? |
| Engineer | Hydda (path), Workshop recruit | Who makes the wall clever? |
| Siege Specialist | Military (path), Catapult bond | Who breaks their machines? |
| Dragon Rider | Drakship (star-gated) | Who owns the sky? |

**Universal legend ladder** (all classes):

```
Village Guard → [Class Identity] → Veteran → Captain → Jarl → High King/Queen
```

## Universal rank responsibilities

| Rank | New responsibilities | Visual evolution | Fortress relationship |
|------|------------------------|------------------|------------------------|
| **Village Guard** | Hold one post; survive 1 assault | Mud-stained gear, no cloak | “Unknown on the wall” |
| **Class Identity** | Class ability active | Class silhouette clear | Preferred post hinted |
| **Veteran** | Talent pick; scar risk | Notched weapon, cloak clasp | Named in roster banner |
| **Captain** | Post aura buffs allies | Banner on back, heavier armor | “Captain of the West Gate” title |
| **Jarl** | Hero spell or signature aura | Gold trim, saga portrait | Chronicle chapter; council seat in Hall |
| **High King** | Legacy rule; succession | Unique portrait, memorial prep | Fortress names a gate or hall wing |

## Relationship rules (all heroes)

1. **Preferred post** shown after Class Identity
2. **Captain+** grants advisor line in Fortress Prep
3. **Jarl+** unlocks hero spell slot (Mage Tower)
4. **Fallen Jarl+** always memorial + legacy option
5. Traits modify **voice**, not only stats — see [TRAITS.md](TRAITS.md)
