# Northern Shield — Player Motivation

*Why players stay — hour by hour, month by month*

Motivation is **emotional first, mechanical second**. This document explains what pulls players forward at each time horizon without introducing new systems. Mechanics referenced exist in [progression_tree.md](progression_tree.md) and [economy_flow.md](economy_flow.md).

---

## Motivation model

Every session balances three pulls:

| Pull | Question | Horizon |
|------|----------|---------|
| **Short-term** | What happens in the next 15 minutes? | Next assault |
| **Mid-term** | What chapter am I finishing? | Age / region arc |
| **Long-term** | What fortress am I building? | Campaign saga |

**Emotional reward** is always one of: relief, pride, attachment, awe, legacy.

---

## First hour (0–60 minutes)

### Why they continue

- Curiosity: *"Can one berserker hold?"*
- Unfinished fear: first assault not yet won or first breach not yet repaired
- Name on the roster — sunk attachment in minute 20

### Long-term goal (seeded)

- Vague sense: *"This might become a kingdom"* from command map silhouette tease
- Not explained — **felt** through skald tone

### Short-term goal

- Survive **The First Night**
- Win first assault; see After Action prose
- Optionally recruit second hero

### Emotional reward

- **Relief** after first victory — exhale gameplay
- **Pride** at naming — *"I made someone"*
- **Fear** at gate redline — hooks return for hour two

### Design guardrails

- No build grid tutorial; one post, one button
- War Camp after victory, not before — reward not homework
- Debrief < 30 seconds readable

---

## First evening (1–4 hours)

### Why they continue

- Roster growing — *"I know all their names"*
- First **scar** on gate or first boss on horizon
- Settlement chapter tease — refugees event
- "One more assault" before bed

### Long-term goal

- Clear **Age II** fantasy: *"Turn camp into settlement"*
- Stone wall shown in blueprint grayed — visible future

### Short-term goal

- Fix breach / recruit third hero
- Clear first **region band** or defeat first mini-boss
- Try second front on command map

### Emotional reward

- **Belonging** — settlement smoke, more voices
- **Responsibility** — food or wood tension first time
- **Competence** — intel + assignment click = win

### Design guardrails

- New system max **one per session** (e.g. wood OR food, not both same hour)
- Advisor explains *why* in one sentence, not patch notes

---

## First weekend (5–20 hours)

### Why they continue

- **Captain promotion** within reach for favourite hero
- Great Hall / stone curtain — visual tier change weekend project
- Chronicle entries worth reading aloud
- Boss south front — community-share moment (*"finally killed the Ash March"*)

### Long-term goal

- **Fortress Age III** — *"I want the stone castle on the map"*
- Favourite hero path to **Jarl** (distant but named in Hall UI)

### Short-term goal

- Stone quarry running; repair vs Hall upgrade choice
- Multi-wave assault chain without changing assignment
- First rune or star purchase that visibly changes hero

### Emotional reward

- **Stewardship** — fortress as property
- **Grief** (controlled) — first meaningful fall
- **Pride** — skyline upgrade screenshot

### Design guardrails

- Weekend player must see **fortress look different** Sat vs Fri
- No mandatory grind wall — boss milestones unblock stone

---

## First month (20–80 hours)

### Why they continue

- **Stronghold** politics — market, research, reputation
- Multiple heroes at Veteran+; composition is **identity**
- Region 50+ pressure — neglected front narrative
- Collection of chronicle chapters — *"my saga"*

### Long-term goal

- **Citadel** — rune walls, spells, dragon tease
- Capstone path choice visible (rune-lord / dragon / memorial)

### Short-term goal

- Finish research branch node before boss week
- Craft named weapon for main hero
- Restore neglected gate facing before coalition event

### Emotional reward

- **Aspiration** — recruits want your banner
- **Anxiety** (good) — can't do everything
- **Mastery** — prep wins without battle UI clutter

### Design guardrails

- Month-one player should quote a **hero name** unprompted
- Glory / cosmetics offer vanity without pay-to-win

---

## 100 hours (endgame + completionists)

### Why they continue

- **Last Bastion** regions — gravity, not grind
- Jarl / High King fantasy completion per class
- Saga export; memorial avenue full
- NG+ optional — same scars, new jarl voice
- Perfect assaults on favourite fronts — self-imposed mastery

### Long-term goal

- **Finish the saga** — region 100 with chosen capstone silhouette
- **Complete chronicle** — all titles, legendary gear set, dragon bond path

### Short-term goal

- Final assault prep — spell loadout, dragon lane, full roster
- Epilogue branch unlock
- Last promotion ceremony

### Emotional reward

- **Legacy** — bittersweet victory
- **Awe** — citadel glow pre-final battle
- **Completion** — book of saga, not achievement pop spam

### Design guardrails

- No infinite vertical power — **payoff** systems
- 100h player sees **scars from hour 1** referenced in epilogue if possible

## Motivation by player type

| Player type | Primary hook | Keep them with |
|-------------|--------------|----------------|
| **Saga reader** | Chronicle, debrief prose | Titles, epitaphs, export |
| **Base builder** | Fortress skyline | Age upgrades, repair visible |
| **Roster parent** | Hero names, ranks | Promotions, gear, legacy |
| **Optimizer** | Prep efficiency | Stars, intel, composition meter — never at cost of story |
| **Explorer** | Command map, events | Discoveries, diplomacy, rare relics |

All types must pass through **The First Night** — attachment is universal.

---

## When motivation breaks (warning signs)

| Symptom | Likely cause | Fix direction |
|---------|--------------|---------------|
| Quit after hour 1 | Grid/TD confusion | Posts not tiles |
| Quit after breach | Feels like fail state | Scar + repair, not game over |
| Mid-game churn | Gold solves all | Wood/stone/rep tension |
| Late-game boredom | Number creep only | Citadel spectacle + saga end |
| Skirmish only | Campaign UI noise | Screen laws enforced |
