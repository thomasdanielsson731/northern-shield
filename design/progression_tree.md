# Northern Shield — Fortress Progression Tree

*Ages I–VI · the backbone of every future feature*

> One Berserker and a wooden palisade → a legendary citadel and High Kings.

Each **Age** is a **chapter**: new silhouette, new systems, new emotions. Complexity emerges **one beat at a time** — never a level-up dump. Ages align with [roadmap.md](roadmap.md) and unlock via [unlock_philosophy.md](unlock_philosophy.md).

**Pacing model:** Civilization’s era shifts · Clash of Clans’ visual base growth · Idle Heroes’ roster depth over time · Era of Chaos’ chapter stakes.

---

## Age overview

| Age | Name | Campaign regions (guide) | Fortress tier | One-word feeling |
|-----|------|--------------------------|---------------|------------------|
| I | Survival | 1–8 | Village | *Exist* |
| II | Settlement | 9–25 | Settlement | *Belong* |
| III | Fortress | 26–50 | Fortress | *Endure* |
| IV | Stronghold | 51–75 | Stronghold | *Rule* |
| V | Citadel | 76–95 | Citadel | *Wonder* |
| VI | Last Bastion | 96–100 + epilogue | Last Bastion | *Legacy* |

Region bands are **guides**, not hard gates — boss victories and Great Hall tier are the real unlock drivers.

## Age I — Survival

### Fortress appearance

- Single **wooden palisade** ring, one gap (west gate)
- Fire pit, lean **watch platform**, buried **treasury chest**
- One longhouse smoke; mud paths; no stone
- Command map shows a **dot**, not a castle

### Available buildings

| Building | Tier | Role |
|----------|------|------|
| Palisade | I | Only wall |
| West Gate (timber) | I | Single post |
| Watch Platform | I | Intel stub |
| Tiny Treasury | I | Gold reserve cap |
| Campfire Hall | I | Roster cap 3 |

*Field siege, smithy, granary — not yet.*

### Available defenders

- **1 named Berserker** (starter) — Village Guard rank
- Optional 2nd recruit after first victory: Valkyrie or Military
- **Cap: 3 heroes** — everyone known by name

### New systems unlocked

- Command map (single front emphasis)
- **One defensive post** mental model → expands to west gate first
- Fortress Prep (minimal): assign hero, begin assault
- Battle gold, lives, gate breach fear
- First chronicle line
- War Camp (roster only — no fortress tab clutter)

### Economy changes

- **Battle:** Gold only — kills and wave clear
- **Campaign:** None yet — repairs cost gold from field
- **Kingdom:** None
- Player learns: gold feeds **today**, not tomorrow’s empire

### Enemy evolution

- Wolf packs, raiders, small **draugr** scouts
- Single portal, single lane pressure
- Low HP, teach **gate focus** not maze tricks
- No siege engines, no flanking portals until Age II

### Player fantasy

*"I am the last adult with a sword. The fire must not go out."*

### Emotional goal

**Fragile hope.** First victory feels impossible; second feels earned. Player bonds with **one hero** before managing an army.

### Chapter transition (→ Age II)

- Clear **Region 8** or upgrade **Campfire Hall → Longhouse**
- Palisade survives with scars OR treasury funded
- Skald line: *"Refugees on the road. We are no longer alone."*

## Age II — Settlement

### Fortress appearance

- Palisade with **stone footings**; second gate **stub** (sealed)
- **Barracks** smoke; **granary** thatch; watch tower on stone base
- Outer path to fields; first banner on pole
- Command map: **four fronts** appear but only two active

### Available buildings

| Building | Notes |
|----------|-------|
| Longhouse / Barracks | Recruit cadence |
| Granary | Food cap introduced |
| Lumber Camp | Wood income |
| North/East gate stubs | Unlock with stone |
| Healing Lodge I | Injury recovery (design) |
| Watch Tower (proper) | Scout post bonuses |

### Available defenders

- Core 4 classes: **Berserker, Valkyrie, Military, Hydda**
- Cap rises to **6**; fortress **roles** unlock (gatekeeper, scout…)
- Traits assigned on recruit or first naming

### New systems unlocked

- **Multi-post** Fortress Prep (8 posts, 6 hero cap on posts)
- **Wood + food** campaign layer
- War Camp **pure** — no battlefield backdrop
- Assault **front intel** (west vs north emphasis)
- Warband composition advisor
- Gate damage **persists** one facing
- Between-assault **events** (caravan, wolf moon)

### Economy changes

- **Battle:** Gold unchanged
- **Campaign:** Wood (repair palisade), Food (roster upkeep 1/assault)
- **Kingdom:** First **Reputation** point from boss — “neighbors know us”
- Tension: **feed** vs **repair**

### Enemy evolution

- Raids from **two portals** (waves 2+ in multi-portal maps)
- Shield-bearer draugr — teaches wall damage
- First **elite** with name in intel card
- Occasional east-front probe (tutorial for multi-front map)

### Player fantasy

*"We are a settlement. People sleep behind our wall."*

### Emotional goal

**Belonging and responsibility.** Roster feels like family; neglecting food or a front has consequence.

### Chapter transition (→ Age III)

- **Stone Curtain** project complete (quarry + boss stone drop)
- Great Hall foundation laid
- Skald: *"They send siege, not raids. Build in stone or build in graves."*

---

## Age III — Fortress

### Fortress appearance

- **Stone curtain wall**, four compass gates
- **Great Hall** visible at center — heart of roster
- Treasury annex, **smithy** chimney, ballista platform scaffold
- Crenellations; banner hall; breach patches visible

### Available buildings

| Building | Notes |
|----------|-------|
| Great Hall I–II | Roster cap 8–10, promotions |
| Treasury II | Reserve + passive gold |
| Smithy | Crafting tier 1 |
| Quarry (meta) | Stone trickle |
| Ballista / Catapult platforms | Siege posts |
| Armory | Equipment slots formalized |
| Rune Shrine (field) | Star → rune bridge |

### Available defenders

- All **6 MVP classes** + star-gated **Isjätten** (boss unlock)
- **Blondie** (archer) via Hall II or reputation
- Cap **10**; promotion to **Veteran** ranks common

### New systems unlocked

- Stone + **iron** economy
- **Chronicle** titles, scars, MVP saga
- Assault chains 2–3 waves; auto-advance
- **After Action** prose-first
- Battle = **execution only** (no campaign build dock)
- Rune equip (stars); fortress upgrade nodes (barracks, wallworks…)
- Deploy snapshot / fallen hero return next assault

### Economy changes

- **Battle:** Gold + star performance
- **Campaign:** Stone (walls, hall), Iron (siege, arms)
- **Kingdom:** Reputation gates **market** blueprints
- Gold cannot buy stone without **quarry** or victory bundles

### Enemy evolution

- **Siege lines** — ram crews target gates
- Multi-wave plans test **same assignment**
- First **named assault** codenames matter emotionally
- Boss on south front — realm stakes

### Player fantasy

*"This is a fortress on the map. Enemies plan campaigns against us."*

### Emotional goal

**Stewardship.** Breach is a **wound** to heal, not a restart. Player plans assaults like a jarl, not a tile placer.

### Chapter transition (→ Age IV)

- Great Hall II + **first diplomatic choice** (aid vs hoard)
- Outer bailey blueprint
- Skald: *"Kings send envoys. The hill is no longer ours alone."*

---

## Age IV — Stronghold

### Fortress appearance

- **Outer bailey** + inner keep
- Market stalls, training yard, **memorial stones**
- Allied flags; repair history on each facing
- War Camp panorama shows **district hints**

### Available buildings

| Building | Notes |
|----------|-------|
| Market | Trade, reputation sinks |
| Scriptorium | Research unlock |
| Forge II | Armor tiers |
| Temple / Ancestor Hall | Faith stub, morale |
| Workshop | Engineering — trap upgrades |
| Healing Lodge II | Injury timers |
| Embassy post | Diplomacy events |

### Available defenders

- **Engineer** (Hydda promotion path or recruit) — siege/trap focus
- **Healer** path (Valkyrie + Temple) — support legend tree
- Veterans promote to **Captain**; jarl candidates emerge
- Squad presets + bond dialogue in debrief

### New systems unlocked

- **Research** (ancient knowledge)
- **Crafting** weapons/armor
- **Reputation** economy full
- Command map **non-combat nodes** (trade, omen)
- Equipment beyond runes — named gear
- Light **diplomacy** (caravan aid, tribute)
- Hero **injuries** between assaults

### Economy changes

- **Battle:** Emergency repair tokens rare — prep matters more
- **Campaign:** Worker assignment (food → quarry/mine)
- **Kingdom:** Ancient knowledge from bosses & scriptorium
- **Relics** — one slot fortress-wide (boss drops)

### Enemy evolution

- **Coordinated assaults** — pressure on neglected front
- Elite heralds, champion duels before boss
- Enemies respond to **reputation** (allied reinforcements reduce wave size)
- Narrative debuffs if food debt

### Player fantasy

*"We are a regional power. Politics is a second battlefield."*

### Emotional goal

**Aspiration and anxiety.** Recruits want **your** banner; ignoring a front hurts in **story**, not only DPS.

### Chapter transition (→ Age V)

- Rune Forge foundation + mythic omen event
- First **rune wall** segment on one facing
- Skald: *"The old magic stirs. The wall can learn spells."*

---

## Age V — Citadel

### Fortress appearance

- **Rune-etched walls**, faint aurora glow
- Spires, ritual circle, **dragon perch** (empty or bonded)
- Living city windows; forge-fires at night
- Sky lane visible on schematic

### Available buildings

| Building | Notes |
|----------|-------|
| Rune Hall / Rune Forge III | Fortress-scale magic |
| Mage Tower | Hero spells, knowledge |
| Dragon Roost | Dragon defense (one bond) |
| Temple II | Faith abilities |
| Ballista III | Rune bolts |

### Available defenders

- Star-gated **Drakship** / dragon rider path
- **Mage** archetype (Blondie + Mage Tower)
- **Jarl** ranks with hero spells
- Cap 10 **legends**, not bodies — quality over quantity

### New systems unlocked

- **Fortress spells** (prep loadout)
- **Hero spells** at Captain/Jarl
- Rune walls; geas costs
- Dragon defense platform
- Mythic assault modifiers
- District visuals (memorial avenue)

### Economy changes

- **Battle:** Temporary boosts costly — kingdom resources
- **Campaign:** Magic upkeep (food + knowledge)
- **Kingdom:** **Glory** from saga milestones — cosmetic + one rule tweak
- Stars tie to **rune** tier, not raw power creep

### Enemy evolution

- **Jötnar**, storm hosts, cursed kings
- Phase bosses (UTGARD standard)
- Assaults test **spell loadout** and dragon lane
- Enemies counter prior player strategies (readable intel)

### Player fantasy

*"Magic returned because we refused to die. The citadel is wonder and terror."*

### Emotional goal

**Awe and sacrifice.** Power costs **something permanent** — geas, dragon appetite, gate scar.

### Chapter transition (→ Age VI)

- Enter **Regions 96–100** — Last War declared in chronicle
- Fortress silhouette **branches** by campaign choices
- Skald: *"If we fall, the age ends. Write the saga now."*

---

## Age VI — Last Bastion

### Fortress appearance

- **Unique capstone silhouette** per path (rune-lord vs dragon-pact vs memorial king)
- Aurora, world-tree motif, named wall segments
- Fallen legends on memorial avenue
- Scarred beauty — never pristine

### Available buildings

- All prior tiers **matured** — no new building types, only **legendary variants**
- Capstone: **High Hall**, **World Anchor** (narrative), **Saga Stone**

### Available defenders

- **High King / High Queen** — one per campaign optional
- Legacy succession — fallen jarl buffs heir
- Full legend trees visible in chronicle export

### New systems unlocked

- **Epilogue world states**
- Saga **book export**
- NG+ optional: new jarl, scarred fortress
- No new combat buttons — **payoff** of all systems

### Economy changes

- Resources converge to **meaningful sinks**: final upgrades, last repairs, victory tribute
- Gold flood prevented — everything is **choice**

### Enemy evolution

- **Final coalition** — all prior enemy themes
- Narrative extinction stakes, not HP sponge only
- Victory condition: **hold + saga complete**

### Player fantasy

*"The last magical citadel. The North stands because of us."*

### Emotional goal

**Legacy.** Player finishes knowing **which Gunnar** they had and **which wall** still bears his axe marks.

---

## Cross-age rules

1. **One new economy layer per age** maximum
2. **One new prep verb per age** (assign → repair → craft → research → spell loadout)
3. **Visual tier change** mandatory at every age boundary
4. **Battle UI complexity** flat — depth off-field only
5. Boss victories = **emotional punctuation**, not only gates
