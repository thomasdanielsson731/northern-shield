RULES:
- If you need to know what's in a file, read it first — do not assume
- game.js has no exports — it is a side-effect module; do not add exports
- New systems go in new files under src/campaign/, src/roster/, etc.
  game.js calls into them; it does not absorb them
- Defender progression state lives on Defender (roster/defender.js)
  Tower mirrors only: _careerLevel, name, defenderId
- saveCampaign() must NEVER be called inside requestAnimationFrame or game tick
- Campaign mode is pathless (no BFS on placement); BFS only on skirmish/non-pathless presets
- Upgrade heroes only in War Camp (canUpgradeHeroLevelBetweenAssaults), not during assault
