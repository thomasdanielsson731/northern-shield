 BALANCE INTENT:
 - Shield Wall: core strategic element — players shape the maze, towers fill it
 - Berserker: anti-swarm, reliable DPS against Graveborn clusters
 - Valkyrie: anti-boss, long range, strong single-target vs Golem
 - Archer: versatile mid-range shooter, affordable early DPS
 - Catapult: high value vs tight chokepoints with clustered enemies
 - Blondie: CC support, lets other towers shoot stunned enemies

 BALANCE GOALS:
 - All tower types should feel viable in different situations
 - Shield Wall should be used more than offensive towers (maze > firepower)
 - Golem requires preparation — players must have Valkyrie placed
 - Wisp punishes tower neglect (bypasses the maze entirely)
 - Banshee punishes tower clustering (EMP takes out groups)
 - Multiple viable playstyles: maze-heavy vs tower-heavy vs hybrid

 Avoid fake complexity. Readable threat levels beat hidden math.
