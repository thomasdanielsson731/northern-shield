BOSS WARNING SYSTEM — Northern Shield

 GOAL:
     When a Golem (boss) is about to spawn, the player must know.
     The warning should build tension without breaking gameflow.

 CURRENT STATE:
     Golem (tank) is in ENEMY_DEFS — high HP, slow, large radius.
     Wave composition shows "Golem xN" in right panel during break.
     No dynamic warning event on boss spawn.

 PROPOSED SYSTEM:

     Pre-wave warning (in break state):
     - If next wave contains Golems: show a red "BOSS INCOMING" banner
     - Banner fades in for 0.5s, holds 2s, fades out
     - Show during countdown phase, not during active combat

     Spawn moment:
     - When a Golem is spawned: flash the portal bright red for 0.3s
     - Screen shake: small impact jolt (3px, 10 frames)
     - Golem entrance: scale from 0.5x to 1.0x over 20 frames (spawn animation)

     Boss health bar:
     - While a Golem is alive: show a health bar above the enemy
     - All enemies have hp/maxHp — use this for the bar
     - Bar color: green > 75%, yellow 50-75%, red < 50%
     - Boss bar is taller (4px instead of 2px) and has a border

 IMPLEMENTATION NOTES:
     - Track 'bossWarningTimer' in game.js during countdown
     - 'boss': true flag in ENEMY_DEFS for Golem (tank)
     - Health bars drawn in Enemy.draw() if boss = true or hp < maxHp

 RULES:
 - Warning banner never covers the grid playarea — show in HUD overlay
 - Boss health bar does not show for regular enemies at full health
 - Keep the warning feeling optional to read — not an interrupting popup
